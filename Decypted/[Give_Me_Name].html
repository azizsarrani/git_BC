About


Real Struggle..

Going to a hospital appointment has always been an annoyance! Wait in a long queue, wait for the doctor, confirm information that should be confirmed already! Lose your data, medical records and the need to confirm your insurance.

The Solution!

All these problems can be solved simply using an IoT system on blockchain, every patient will have a card with all information and medical record. Once the patient enters the hospital gate the counter will get notified about the patient and can respond effectivly by sending the medical record to the doctor and prepare the needed medical tests.

Insurance Role?

The whole process of going to the hospital, meeting doctor, medical tests and medicine costs will be available for the insurance company. This will save time, money and even your LIFE!
